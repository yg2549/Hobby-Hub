Long-Distance Clubs

Remote clubs are designed for everyone and anyone regardless of where they live as a way to connect even from afar. They combine both individual activities and virtual group activities. The time commitment and pressue is minimal due to time differences and other constraints, but these remote clubs allow a sense of community while being able to manage ones own schedule.

Our current remote clubs:

Format: Set reading goals for a single book with group discussions through zoom and messaging.

Less commitment option: Members can send a response if they can’t attend live meetings.

Format: Individual crafting session with a virtual show-and-tell of the project at the end.

More commitment option: Can join online meetings to craft "together" and give/recieve advice between sessions.

Format: Watch party services to watch together while on a zoom call and discuss after.

More commitment option: Can also watch a show and watch episodes individually and discuss after or during a watch party.
