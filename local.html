In-Person Clubs

Local groups focus on consistent meetups with clear logistics. Event cards include time, location, and what to bring, making it easy for participants to show up and participate.

Our current in-person clubs

Run Club

Group weekly long runs and shorter meetups for anyone training or just building consistency. Pace-friendly, supportive and long runs are based on a 6-month marathon training plan.

Hike Club

Weekend hikes with a pre-determined difficulty level and meeting point. Carpool is also planned in advance for further hikes.

Game Night Club

Midweek game nights with snacks and drinks. Games varry from classic board games, card games, creative games like DnD, or anything anyone suggest.
