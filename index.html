How it works

The platform focuses on small groups that are driven by a joint interest and that also prioritize activity. HobyHub supports two primary modes of engagement, long-distance hobby groups and in-person activities, so that no matter where you are and where your loved ones are, you can always find a way to connect with them over your shared interets. You simply have to decide which mode you are interested in participating in and pick the hobbies that you are excited to find community with!

Long-Distance Clubs

Individual participation in the clubs hobby coupled with remote sessions that follow prompts, check-ins and sometimes virtual communal participation in the hobbies.

In-Person Clubs

Consistent meetups as well as indiviual participation and sharing when meating up.
